I found this series of questions/prompts in a pile of paper. Not sure where they came from:

  1. How did writing about the project inform the design?
  2. What is the call to action? Is it BOLD? Consider the context of the issue, what might bold mean for your issue?
  3. How does the designer invite participation? what visual language or material cues do they give the use of the toolkit?
  4. If you were to add another digital or physical tool to the kit, what would it be?
  5. How would the toolkit be distributed or shared? online? at an event? on a bulletin board?
